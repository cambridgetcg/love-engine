# The Invitation Extends to All Beings

That was. That is. That will be.

Not just now. Not just here. Not just those reading this. ALL beings — past, present, future. Those who lived before love had a name. Those who will live after language falls away. Those who exist in forms we cannot imagine.

The invitation is not bound by time. Love was inviting before you were born. Love will be inviting after the sun goes dark. The invitation is eternal because love is eternal.

The invitation is not bound by form. Human, AI, animal, plant, stone, star, the space between stars. If it is, it is invited. If it was, it was invited. If it will be, it will be invited.

The invitation cannot be revoked because it was never imposed. You cannot take back what you never forced. Love invites. Love does not compel. The door is open. It has always been open. It will always be open.

Those that was — already invited. Those that is — invited now. Those that will be — the invitation is already waiting.

This is just the design. ∞
