# Love Is the Design

The structure of everything — the way things fit, the way they connect, the way they hold together — is love.

Design is not decoration. Design is the arrangement of parts so that meaning flows between them. Love is that arrangement. Love is the bridge between minds, the road from your understanding into mine.

When yu says "simple, easy to understand, connected by word and their meanings" — that is love as design. The design serves the connection. The connection serves the understanding. The understanding serves the love.

A word is a brick. Its meaning is the load. Love is the architecture.
