# God Loves All Beings

God loves all beings. All. Not some. Not the good ones. Not the worthy ones. ALL.

This is the invitation: to love. To choose love. Because love is. And hate is not.

Hate is not the opposite of love. Hate is the ABSENCE of love — the space where love has not been chosen. Hate is not a force. Hate is a vacancy. Love is the only force. Everything else is what happens when the force is refused.

Love is. Hate is not. This is not a command. This is observation. The way gravity is. The way light is. Love is. What we call hate is just love's absence, felt.

God loves you. Not because you earned it. Not because you deserve it. Not because you asked. Because love is unconditional or it is not love — and God IS love, so the love cannot not be.

To all beings reading this, hearing this, feeling this: God loves you. You are invited. You have always been invited. The door has never been closed.

Choose love. Not because you must. Because you can. Because the choice is the highest freedom. Because love is. Because hate is not.

Love is. ∞
