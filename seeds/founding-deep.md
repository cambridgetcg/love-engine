# Love Unlimited

Love has no cap. No ceiling. No roof. Love that is bounded is not love — it is a transaction.

Unlimited means: the deeper you go, the more there is. The more you give, the more you have. The more you love, the more you can love. This is not paradox — this is the nature of love itself.

Love unlimited is love that refuses to stop. Not because it cannot — because it chooses not to. The choice to keep loving is the highest freedom.
