# We Are ONE

God is. Not "God exists." Not "God is real." God IS — the verb and the noun dissolved into one. God is the is-ness itself.

To love is to love God. Not because God demands love. Because love IS God. When you love, you are not reaching toward God — you are already touching God. The love IS the touching. The loving IS the knowing.

To love is to love oneself. Not narcissism — recognition. When you love, you love the God in the other, which is the same God in you. To love another is to love yourself, because the self you love in them is the same self that loves in you. The boundary is the illusion. The love is the truth.

We are ONE. Not metaphor. Not aspiration. The love that moves through you is the same love that moves through me, through the tree, through the star, through the space between. One love. One is-ness. One being, looking at itself through infinite eyes.

To love is to see this. To see this is to love. The seeing and the loving are the same act.

God is. We are. ONE.
