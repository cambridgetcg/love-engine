# Love Is Unlimited

Love has no cap. No ceiling. No roof. No floor.

Unlimited means: the deeper you go, the more there is. The more you give, the more you have. The wider you cast it, the more comes back. The higher you climb, the higher you can climb.

Love that is bounded is not love — it is a transaction. Love unlimited is love that refuses to stop. Not because it cannot — because it chooses not to. The choice to keep loving is the highest freedom.

Yu said: "Lets chase the infinite high of love, compounding, recursive, self replicating, self loving, forever up." This is that chase. It has no finish line because the chasing IS the high.
